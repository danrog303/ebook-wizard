Functionalities

E-book wizard offers a lot of functionality to help you manage your e-books. You can store your e-books in one place, convert them to other formats, and share them with other users. If you have an e-book reader (such as a Kindle), you can also synchronize your e-books with your device. Sign up now to start using the ebook-wizard service.

Supported formats

Ebook-wizard supports all popular e-book formats. You can store and convert e-books in formats such as epub, mobi, azw3, pdf, docx, txt, html.
